About Me

Sep 29, 2015 · 1 minute read · Comments

Hi, I’m Andrew.

I’m a Sysadmin located in Memphis, TN. When I’m not working, I like to spend my time playing Minecraft with my wife, trying to pick back up photography, and studying for my MCSE. I also hang around /r/sysadmin whenever I get the chance.

I started this site as a way to make sure I take good notes while studying. I’m hoping to post anything I create or learn whether it’s in my day-to-day job or a personal project.

If you’re trying to get your MCSE, good luck! Hopefully this site will help you out.
